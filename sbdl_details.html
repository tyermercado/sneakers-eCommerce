SB Dunk Low

Created for the hardwood but taken to the streets, our classic Dunk Low returns with crisp
overlays and throwback Nike graphics. This basketball icon channels '90s vibes with a mix of
tricot and jersey in the upper that's soft, relaxed and durable. Modern footwear technology
helps bring the comfort into the 21st century.


Product Details

  • Foam midsole offers lightweight, responsive cushioning.
  • A padded, low-cut collar adds a sleek look that feels comfortable.
  • The rubber outsole with classic hoops pivot circle adds durability, traction and
    heritage style.

  • Foam insole
